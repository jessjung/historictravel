About project

길 따라 역사여행(Korean historic travel app) lets you get inspirations and plan to visit thousands of Korean historical monuments and legacies from its 5000 years long history. Also, it aims to offer a map-based data visualization and multi-media contents in order to draw users to hands-on trip experiences.

This project is presented at Startup Idea Competition using Open API 2014, sponsored by Korean government. It is one of 10 finalists out of 374 ideas and waiting for its final presentation on November 25th.

About website

This website is created by Jess for personal project documentation, who is responsible for UX/UI/Visual design for this app.

UX design

Main features of this mobile app is to search venue by keyword, address and name, to create a custom trip plan by adding venues and to share and sneak well-organized trip plans.
